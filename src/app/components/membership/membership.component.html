General Membership

General Membership shall be open to well established and distinguished businessmen and senior professionals who are Indian Citizens or persons of Indian Origin holding a valid residence or employment in the Ras Al Khaimah. The membership will be by invitation, and all membership applications will be approved by the Membership Screening Committee and Governing Board.

Honorary Membership

Honorary Membership shall be open to persons who are distinguished members of the business and professional community and in the opinion of the Governing Council have a genuine and serious interest in the aims and objectives of the Council and a close association or relationship with the Indian Business or professional Community in the UAE. Honorary Members shall not be required to pay any Membership fee and shall not be qualified to be the officers or members of the Governing body and shall not have any voting rights and their presence shall not be counted for quorum in any general meeting.

Corporate Membership

Corporate Membership shall be open to companies established in RAK whose ownership and/or senior management and control is held by Indian Citizens or persons of Indian origin. Each Corporate Member shall be entitled to appoint two members of its senior management to be its duly appointed representatives in all matters related to the Council. The appointment of such representatives or their replacement(s) shall be subject to the prior approval of the Governing Board.

Membership Benefits
  • IBPC assists its members in interacting with the government departments in RAK in the smooth conduct of their business and profession.
  • IBPC provides a platform for Indian Businessmen & Professionals to collectively address issues of mutual interest and concern.
  • IBPC provides network opportunity for promoting the business and profession through interaction with other members. Invitation to all IBPC events like seminar, symposium, business networking, and so on.
  • Interaction with visiting trade delegations.
Membership subscription fees
Membership will be against the onetime payment of an of amount AED 1,000/- for General Membership and AED 2,000/- for Corporate Membership and shall be considered as a fee which will remain in the IBPC Bank Account. In addition to this, individual members shall pay an additional amount of AED 200/- and corporate members shall pay AED 500/- as annual fee to meet costs of IBPC RAK meetings etc. The fee structure shall be reviewed by the Governing Council and revised as may be considered appropriate as per by-laws of the Council.
