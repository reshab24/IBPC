Who are we?

Indian Business & Professional Council (IBPCRAK) established in 2012 is a non-profit body of
Indian businessmen and professionals based in Ras Al Khaimah in particular and U.A.E in general.
IBPC RAK is having patronage from Indian embassy in Abu Dhabi and
Consulate General of India, Dubai. IBPCRAK facilitates Indian Business & Professional community,
in whatever ways possible to augment trade and commerce between the nations.

What is our strength?

IBPCRAK draws its strength from its members and in turn provides a platform for airing their views on matters of mutual business interests. It also guides the members to resolve their difficulties by pooling the collective experience and connectivity.

What do we represent?

We represent Indian Business & Professional Community in the emirates before the Government and non Governmental authorities to address individual problem or problems of a trade or to solve general problems of trade and industries of India & U.A.E

What do we promote?

To promote trade, investment, tourism, social interactions between India and UAE particularly in RAK and UAE in general by with government authorities, trade and commerce bodies in India and UAE.

How we help you grow?

By arranging meetings, seminars, workshops to develop trade and commerce for the benefits of its members in particular and trade and Industry in general.

What do we facilitate?

We facilitate Indian Business & Professional community in whatever way possible to promote trade and commerce

What makes us non-profit organization?

Our objective is to give benefit and in the interest of the members of the council. This makes us nonprofit entity.
