Governing Council ( GC ) shall comprise of 11 Members and shall serve for two years. Thereafter, the GC shall be elected at the Annual General meeting every 2 years, which shall be held three months before the term of the existing board is expiring. The GC shall elect President,Vice President, Secretary General, Joint Secretary General, Treasurer and Joint Treasurer, by a simple majority of votes from among the members of the Governing Council The office bearers shall have a term for two years. The GC shall have the right to nominate and co-opt not more than two additional members to the Council provided that such additional members have not contested the election for the relevant term and the members of the Council agree unanimously to such nomination and co-option. The co-opted members shall not have the right to vote at any meeting of the Governing Council.
